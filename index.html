The Oblivion Crisis

On a night without omen, assassins cut down Uriel Septim VII in the tunnels beneath the Imperial City, and the Dragonfires that warded the mortal world went dark. Above every province the sky tore open, and burning gates spilled the legions of Mehrunes Dagon onto the soil of Cyrodiil.

Out of a forgotten cell walked a prisoner with no name and no past, carrying the last wish of a dying Emperor — find the lost heir, and shut the jaws of Oblivion.
